PUBLICATION


Author : Alang Artha Iwana
Supervisor : Raphael Bianco Huwae & Andy Hidayat Jatmika
I have an interest in cybersecurity, particularly in identifying risks before they become problems. I have channeled this interest into a scientific publication titled “Threat Modeling Using the STRIDE and DREAD Approaches.”
In this project, we systematically analyzed an educational service system to identify potential security vulnerabilities. We used the STRIDE method to classify threats and the DREAD method to assess their risk levels. Based on the analysis, we successfully identified 18 high-priority risks and formulated effective mitigation strategies to build a more resilient and secure system for users. This project honed my technical skills in security analysis and my critical thinking abilities to protect data and users.

SHORT MOVIE


Tittle : PULANG
Production by Group 4 DKV Universitas Bumi Gora
As the lead actor in the campus short film titled “Pulang,” I had the opportunity to delve into the character of Al, a young boy caught between youth and responsibility. This project is a narrative exploration of family dynamics, sacrifice, and regret.

The story centers on Al's relationship with his mother, a hardworking woman who struggles alone to provide for her son. Through Al's role, I aimed to convey the hidden feelings of longing and loneliness behind his daily routine at school. One of the greatest challenges was portraying the climactic moment when Al realizes his mother has passed away, a scene that demanded a deep exploration of grief and loss.
Working on this project taught me how to build a strong story through simple visual details, such as a letter on the dining table or an unanswered phone call. “Pulang” is not just an acting project, but also a valuable lesson about how a story can touch the hearts of audiences with universal themes of family and time that cannot be turned back.
