"The Best Teacher is Our Own Life Journey"

Don't set your expectations too high.
And don't dwell on things that will weigh on your mind.
Everything will work out in due time.
If you work quickly, you will finish quickly, and vice versa.

Hello, I am Alang Artha Iwana. I am a logical problem-solver, a graduate of Computer Science from Mataram University trained to tackle complex issues. On the other hand, the stage is my second home, where I learn to understand humanity through the art of acting.
My journey has been shaped by these two seemingly different worlds. Since middle school, I discovered a passion for acting, a world that taught me about empathy, expression, and courage.
My experience leading the theater club in high school, portraying various characters from a father to a madman, trained me to adapt and communicate effectively in front of an audience. This confidence led me to bigger stages, including starring as the lead actor in a campus film project titled “PULANG.”
However, I am also challenged by the precise world of technology. During my university years, I not only delved into programming logic but also honed my organizational and leadership skills in the Association of Computer Science Students (HMIF). As a member of the Organizational Division, I learned to manage events, solve problems critically (critical thinking), and act as a reliable mediator, skills that turned out to be not too different from directing a performance.
It may seem contrasting, but for me, code and scripts share similarities in that both require a deep understanding of the audience, a well-structured framework, and solid team collaboration. My experiences in OSIS and HMIF built a foundation of responsibility and time management, while the theater stage gave me the ability to “read the room” and convey ideas in a convincing manner.
Although my academic focus prevented me from participating in formal internship programs, I believe that my life and organizational experiences have provided me with unique advantages. I learned resilience at an early age, raised by my mother's large family, who taught me to be independent and calm in the face of any challenge.
Currently, I am eager to merge both worlds. I am ready to channel my technical problem-solving abilities and communication skills into a career in the tech industry. I actively develop myself through various platforms and plan to attend a bootcamp to deepen my skills, with the goal of building a meaningful and financially independent career.

I believe that every challenge is an opportunity to grow. And I am ready for the next role.
